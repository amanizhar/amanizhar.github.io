Ratings and Impact

Uncharted 3: Drake's Deception was well recieved by critics and users. It was highly ranked and was awarded with numerous awards.

The game was rated by the following media outlets

  • 5/5 by Common Sense Media
  • 9/10 by IMDb
  • 9/10 by GameSpot
  • 92/100 by MetaCritic
  • 8.4/10 by MetaCritic Users

The game was alocated with the following awards

  • VGX Award for Best PS3 Game
  • VGX Award for Best Graphics
  • Writers Guild of America Award for Achievement in Videogame Writing
